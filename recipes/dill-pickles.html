Dill Pickles

Back to Home Page

Description

An easy and delicious recipe for canning delicious dill pickles at home.

Ingredients

For 7 quarts:

  • 1 quart white vinegar
  • 3 quarts water
  • 1 cup salt
  • 14 heads dill
  • 7 cloves garlic

Steps

  1. Wash and despine cucumbers - if you don't despine them, you won't get good pickles.
  2. Soak pickles overnight in ice water.
  3. Rinse, drain, and pack pickles in sterile quart jars.
  4. Pack dill and garlic in the jars with the pickles.
  5. Heat brine and pour over the pickles, filling each jar. Seal jars appropriately.
  6. Leave, sealed, for a few weeks before eating.
